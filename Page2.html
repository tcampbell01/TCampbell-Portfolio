Bio: Teresa Campbell is a web developer, musician, advocate, instructional designer, and experienced instructor specializing in working with immigrant students and students with traumatic backgrounds. She has taught violin and orchestra to children and adults, English as a Second Language to adults, GED coaching, and citizenship preparation, and has also worked in the areas of youth homelessness and crisis work. Teresa holds a Bachelor of Music in violin performance, a Master of Music in Music Education, a second Master of Arts in English as a Second Language, and will graduate with a certificate in Web Development in May 2022. In addition to her passion for web development, Teresa works as an Adult Education Coordinator at the Hartford Public Library, designs asynchronous courses for professional development for educators, maintains a small studio of violin and viola students, plays gigs in the Hartford area, and holds the position of Regional Vice Chair for Region 1 on the Board of American Mensa.
